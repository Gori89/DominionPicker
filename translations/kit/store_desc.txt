Start your game of Dominion with this handy shuffler.
&#8226; Includes all known cards as of April 2015 (Base, Adventures, Alchemy, Cornucopia, Dark Ages, Guilds, Hinterlands, Intrigue, Prosperity, Seaside and Promo sets).
&#8226; Shuffles cards you choose to determine your supply.
&#8226; Recommends if you should play with Platinum, Colonies or Shelters.
&#8226; Includes a Black Market shuffler, for use with that promo card.
&#8226; Remembers your shuffles and favourite games.
&#8226; Ad free
&#8226; Open source. Help the developer improve the app on the GitHub page!<br/>
